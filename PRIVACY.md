# Asli Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: March 7, 2026**

Your privacy is the core of **Asli**. This extension is built to be
"Privacy-First," meaning we don't want your data, we don't need your data, and
we don't have your data.

### 1. Data Collection & Usage

**Asli** does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).

- **Browsing History**: We do not collect, store, or share your browsing
  history. While the extension reads the URL of the active tab to verify if it
  is on our safety list, this verification happens **entirely on your local
  browser**. No URLs are ever transmitted to any external server.
- **User Action Tracking**: We do not track clicks, mouse movements, or
  keystrokes.
- **Permissions**: The "tabs" permission is used only to compare the current
  website domain against a locally stored list of official and malicious
  domains.

### 2. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any data with third parties. There is no user
data to share in the first place.

### 3. Changes to this Policy

If we ever change how the extension works in a way that impacts your privacy, we
will update this policy and notify you through an extension update.
